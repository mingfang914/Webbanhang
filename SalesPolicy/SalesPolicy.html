4. Refunds and Returns

4.1 Return Period

Customers have 30 days from the date of purchase to request a return or refund. Items must be in their original condition with all packaging and documentation intact.

4.2 Non-Returnable Items

  • Perishable goods
  • Customized or personalized products
  • Items with broken seals or used packaging
  • Final sale or clearance items

4.3 Refund Process

  1. Submit a return request through your account
  2. Receive a prepaid return shipping label
  3. Ship the item back to our warehouse
  4. Refund processed within 5-7 business days after receipt

4.4 Refund Amount

Refunds include the purchase price minus any applicable restocking fees (up to 20%). Original shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.
